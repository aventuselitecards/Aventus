# Aventus Elite Cards Website

A professional sports card business website.

## Quick Deploy (Netlify - Free)

1. Go to [Netlify Drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop)
2. Drag the `aventuselitecards` folder onto the page
3. Your site will be live in seconds!

## Connect Your Domain (aventuselitecards.com)

After deploying, Netlify will give you a "DNS Target". Here's what to do:

### Option 1: Point GoDaddy to Netlify (Recommended)

1. In your Netlify dashboard, go to **Domain Settings**
2. Add your domain: `aventuselitecards.com`
3. Netlify will provide nameserver addresses
4. In GoDaddy:
   - Go to your domain → **DNS**
   - Change nameservers to what Netlify gives you
   - Save and wait up to 24 hours

### Option 2: Use A Record + CNAME

1. In GoDaddy DNS, add these records:
   - **A Record**: `@` → Netlify IP (Netlify will show this)
   - **CNAME**: `www` → your-site-name.netlify.app

## Custom Email

The site shows `info@aventuselitecards.com` — you can set this up through:
- **Google Workspace** (paid)
- **Cloudflare Email** (free)
- **Forwarding** through GoDaddy
